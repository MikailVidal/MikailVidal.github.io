Who am I?

I'm Mikail Vidal, an upcoming Data Scientist

As an aspiring data scientist studying at FHNW in Aargau, Switzerland, I stand at the intersection of technology and innovation. My fascination with machine learning drives my academic journey, as I believe it represents the cutting edge of technological advancement.

In my studies, I focus on exploring data, building machine learning models, and drawing insights from complex datasets. I’ve developed skills in data analysis, visualization, and problem-solving, and I thrive on the challenges that come with translating data into impactful solutions. From collecting raw data to delivering clear insights, I’m motivated by the opportunity to make data-driven contributions.

Outside of my academic work, I often take on additional projects, whether it’s exploring coding challenges or diving into the latest industry trends.
When I'm not sitting behind a computer, I like to spend my time with moving my body instead. I'm a huge sports fanatic, actively participating in club sports like gymnastics or playing basketball with friends. Sometimes you can find me on the break-dance floor as well.
